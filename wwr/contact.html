Rapids Rafting

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver the most exhilerating experience you can have on the waterways. With safety at the top of our prorities we will take you on an adventure that you will never forget.

History

For 20 years, we have been taking people on high adventures, through the states' most beautiful rapids and cavernous waterways. The rapids we tour through have a beautiful gold-rush history. The beauty and history of the area makes this a beautiful and educational experience. Because of the area and our expertise, we have become world famous. People travel here, above all others, to take in the beauty of the Rapids Rafting Park and experience the thrill of the waters that is beyond compare.
